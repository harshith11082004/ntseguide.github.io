NTSE

Step towards your future

NTSE stands for National Talent Search Examination.It is a scholarship based exam conducted in a two-tier system.The first tier of the exam is conducted by the state board and the second tier of the exam is conducted by the NCERT council.The scholarship under the present scheme of ntse is awarded to 2000 students around India. Scholarships awarded are as follows: *Scholarship of 1250 rupees per month for class 11 and 12 *Scholarship of 2000 rupees per month for UG and PG.
